Recollections

by Neptuo

The Idea

Core of the recollections app are entries. When you experience something you want memorized, create an entry. Then enrich it with additional information that unlocks more views.

Timeline

Timeline is the default view of your entries. See your recollections as the time goes.

Map

Add map pins to your entries to unlock map view of your entries. This view shows you where have you been, which countries you visited.

Share

Share content with other users. All of the items you add, can be shared with other. You can share with concrete other user or make an item publicly visible.

Media

Upload photos, videos and audio recordings to your entries to make your recollections more visual.

Stories

Have more entries that belongs together? Use story to organize them. Add description, chapters and attach entries.

Beings

Tag other people, animals and well, whoever or whatever you want in entries. Then see recollections you experienced with each of them.

Calendar

Calender gives you month and year overview of entries and let's you jump to day, month or year and see what happens than.

Search

Can't find your entry in other views? Try to look through the content.

Connections

You can connect with users and share individual entries, stories or beings with individual users or publicly with anybody.
